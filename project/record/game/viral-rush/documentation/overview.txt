#feature-title(DESCRIPTION)

<b>Viral Rush</b> is a survival action game where players control a cellular warrior navigating through infected zones to eliminate hostile viruses. The objective is to cleanse each area, gather biological resources, and strengthen the cell to survive increasingly dangerous environments.
@space(0|15)
The game focuses on fast-paced combat, strategic movement, and progressive expansion. By defeating enemies and collecting resources, players unlock upgrades and new lands, gradually reclaiming the cellular world from viral threats.
